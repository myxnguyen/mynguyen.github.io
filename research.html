Research

My research interests lie in the intersection of international economics and data science, with a particular focus on international trade, international macroeconomics, trade policy and global finance.

Cross-border Acquisitions and Network Effects in Trade X

Status: Ongoing | with Marcus Biermann

We investigate the causal effect of foreign acquisitions on the market access of acquired affiliates, using rich datasets at firm-level for German firms operating in service sectors.

Applications of NLP in PTAs

Status: Ongoing

This project aims to understand the heterogenous impacts of Preferential Trade Agreements (PTAs) on trade flows among ASEAN countries with text-data approach.
